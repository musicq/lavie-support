About Lavie

Lavie is a personal finance app that helps you track income, expenses, and savings goals. Set your goals, add your income and expenses, and Lavie calculates the rest — one clear answer: are you on track?

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I add a recurring expense?
    Tap the + button, choose "Add Expense", select "Recurring", pick your frequency, and save.
  • Can I use multiple currencies?
    Yes. Lavie supports multi-currency entries with automatic FX rate updates.
  • How does goal tracking work?
    Set a target amount and deadline. Lavie compares your monthly net cash flow against what's needed and tells you if you're on track.
  • Is my data stored securely?
    All data is stored locally on your device by default. You can optionally enable iCloud sync for cross-device access.
  • How do I export my data?
    Go to Settings → Export to download your financial records.
